About this MVP

This map displays the results of AI-powered land use classification using:

  • Data Source: Sentinel-2 satellite imagery
  • AI Model: Random Forest classifier
  • Classes: Water (blue), Forest (green), Agriculture (yellow)
  • Technology: Python, scikit-learn, Leaflet.js
